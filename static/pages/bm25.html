what's BM25 doing differently?

Two upgrades over TF-IDF: (1) saturation — the 50th time a word appears in a doc helps way less than the 2nd; (2) length normalization — long docs don't automatically win just because they have more chances to contain your words. Both are tuned by the k1 and b knobs.
